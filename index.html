FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Is this space only for men?

Yes — it's a male-centred space designed to respond to the social pressures and cultural norms many men face. Allies and partners may be invited to specific events.

Are sessions confidential?

All one-to-one sessions and support groups follow strict confidentiality guidelines. We can share full privacy terms on request.

Do I need to be ‘in crisis’ to join?

No — many members join to improve routine, build friendships, or manage stress. Crisis support is available 24/7 if needed.
